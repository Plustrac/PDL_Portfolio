About me

Hi, I'm Pierre, and welcome to my professional corner. My educational journey has been a global adventure, with all my schooling up to high school completed in international institutions. Recently, I proudly graduated from both the École Nationale Supérieure d’Arts et Métiers ParisTech and the Georgia Institute of Technology in the United States, shaping my academic foundation with a blend of diverse perspectives.

In the professional realm, my career has been a dynamic expedition across continents and industries. I've served as a Project Manager for Ekoï in Féjus (France), honing my innovation skills. As an Assistant Project Manager for TIME Bicycles, I navigated the complexities of remote work, connecting the factory in Gajary (Slovakia) with the headquarters in Little Rock (USA). My role as an R&D engineer for Composite Jazz (3T Bikes) in Presezzo (Italy) showcased my technical expertise, while supervising the production line for Karl Hess-Plastivaloire in Neunkirchen (Germany) provided hands-on manufacturing experience. Finally, as an Assistant Project Manager for TransNav in New Baltimore, MI (USA) I contributed to impactful global projects.

Beyond the professional area, my heart lies in sports and exploration. In sports, I've achieved notable milestones, such as being the U24 Aquathlon World vice champion or winning the French National Championships in roller hockey. Additionally, my love for traveling has taken me to more than 55 countries and led me to live in Brazil, France, Slovakia, South Korea, the USA, Italy, and Mexico. From trekking adventures covering thousands of kilometers to hitchhiking iconic routes like the Transatlantic, the Trans-Siberian, or even the Highlands, my passion for exploration knows no bounds.
